 FAQ- Galaxy Watch Classic 4

Why is my watch not charging?

Please ensure the wireless charger is connected to a working wall outlet.

Please ensure the watch is properly placed on the wireless charging pad.

How do I turn on/ off the watch?

To turn off the watch, press both buttons on the right side, till a red button pops up that says "Turn off watch?". Tap the button  and the watch will turn off.

If you want to turn on the wartch, press the upper right button for 5 seconds and the watch will turn on. 

The battery drains too fast. What can I do?

Turn off the "always on display". You can do this by swiping down and tapping the icon that looks like a watch.

You can also enable "power saving"mode. This can be enabled by swiping down, tapping the cog-wheel icon to get to settings, and scrolling down for the battery option. When you tap the battery option, the "power saver mode"  will be set to default option of Off. Tap it and turn it on.

Why is there no audio when receiving a phone call?

When inside the call, turn the watch dial to control the volume. Turn the dial to the right, increases the volume, turning it to the left will decrease the volume. Also check the bluetooth settings on your phone, to ensure bluetooth audio for the watch is enabled.

My watch said "It's disconnected" and vibrates. Why?

The bluetooth range for the watch is approximately 20 feet. If the watch is further than 20 feet from your phone, it will disconnect and alert on the watch screen. Once you return back within range, the watch will automatically reconnect with the phone.

How can I sync my watch?

The watch is synced automatically once a connection is established with the phone.

How can I accept a phone call?

When a call comes in, both your phone and watch will start ringing. To accept the call on the phone, simply swipe up the green receiver icon. The call will connect and you can start talking by holding the watch up to your face.

How can I update the watch?

To update the watch, swipe down on the watch screen, tap the cog wheel icon to get into settings, and scroll down to the software update icon. Click the icon and follow the prompts to update the watch. Alternatively, go to your phone, look for the Galaxy Watch app. Then click on Watch settings and scroll down to the Software update option. Follow the prompts.
