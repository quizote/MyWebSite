How to signup on RobinHood

 

Step 1.

On your computer, enter robinhood.com in your browser’s address bar and enter. A screen as the one below opens. Click on the green Sign-Up button

 

Step 2.

Enter first name, last name, email address and create a password with a minimum length of 10 characters.

Click Continue.

Step3.

Enter phone number, residential address, city, state, zip.

Click Continue

Step 4.

Verify phone number. Click on the black “send by text” button .

 

Step 5.

Robinhood will send a 6 digit verification code to be entered on the next screen as seen below

Click Continue.

Step 6.

Enter your Social Security Number, Date of Birth and Citizenship. Click Continue and Robinhood then creates the account.
